# Privacy Policy for SurgeryPreview

**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SurgeryPreview ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website surgerypreview.co (the "Site") and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our Site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Fill out our consultation request form
- Contact us via email or phone
- Sign up for our newsletter
- Participate in surveys or promotions

This information may include:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location preferences
- Procedure interests
- Photos (optional, with your consent)
- Medical history notes (optional)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring URLs
- Pages viewed
- Time spent on pages
- Clickstream data

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
We may receive information about you from:
- Plastic surgeons in our network
- Marketing partners
- Social media platforms
- Publicly available sources

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Match you with qualified plastic surgeons
- Send you information about matched surgeons
- Improve our services and user experience
- Communicate with you about your inquiry
- Send marketing communications (with your consent)
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 With Plastic Surgeons
When you submit a consultation request, we share your information with:
- Up to 3 board-certified plastic surgeons
- Based on your procedure and location preferences
- For the purpose of scheduling consultations

### 4.2 Service Providers
We may share information with third-party vendors who perform services for us, such as:
- Email service providers (SendGrid)
- Database management (Airtable)
- Analytics providers (Google Analytics)
- Payment processors (future implementation)

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:
- Court orders or legal process
- Government requests
- To protect our rights or property
- To prevent illegal activities

### 4.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Secure storage of sensitive information
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:
- Fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

Photos uploaded by users are deleted within 24 hours unless explicit consent is given for longer retention.
