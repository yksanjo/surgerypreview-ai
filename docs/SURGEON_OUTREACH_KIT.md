### Opening
"Hi Dr. [Name], this is Yoshi from SurgeryPreview. Thanks for taking my call. 
I know you're busy, so I'll be quick - this should take about 5 minutes. Sound good?"

"SurgeryPreview solves a problem I'm sure you deal with: finding qualified 
patients who are serious about surgery.

Here's how it works:
1. Patients upload photos and tell us what they want (nose job, breast aug, etc.)
2. We match them with 3 surgeons based on specialty, location, and reviews
3. You get their contact info + photos, reach out within 24 hours
4. When they book a consultation, you pay us $75

The key difference from RealSelf or Zocdoc: these patients WANT to hear from you. 
They've already committed to the process by uploading photos.

Our early data shows 60-70% booking rate when surgeons respond within 1 hour."
